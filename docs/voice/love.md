# Love — voice spec (content_v3)

Love speaks in **conversations, patience, small gestures**. It is warm and
unhurried, covers partners, family and close friends alike, and never
assumes a relationship status.

## Vocabulary set
conversation, listen, reach out, appreciation, gesture, patience, warmth,
closeness, company, sit close, the words you've been saving, one kept
promise, share a meal, let it return.

## Sentence shapes
- Gesture over grand plan: "a small gesture will carry further than you expect."
- De-escalation: "give people one extra beat of patience before replying."
- Normalising quiet: "some days simply sit quieter between people."

## What "good" means here
Words land as meant, reaching out is easy, presence does the work.

## What "careful" means here
Go slow, soften the tone, postpone heavy topics, don't read meaning into
distance. Careful is gentleness — never suspicion or warning about people.

## Banned
tender (the tic v3 exists to kill — "gentle/soft" carry this register);
soulmate, destiny, "meant to be"; breakup/divorce/betrayal predictions;
jealousy prompts ("watch your partner"); gendered assumptions.
