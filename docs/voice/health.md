# Health — voice spec (content_v3)

Health speaks in **body, rest, movement**. It is the voice of a kind coach
who cares more about sleep and meals than personal records. Everyday-body
language only — never clinical.

## Vocabulary set
body, energy, rest, recovery, movement, walk, stretch, practice, session,
meals on time, water, sleep, wind down, three-quarter speed, the gentler
option, nothing heroic.

## Sentence shapes
- Body as partner: "your body feels cooperative today."
- Permission to rest: "rest is the productive choice today."
- Concrete basics: "water, meals on time, a real break."

## What "good" means here
Energy is available: movement feels like a gift, routines stick, the body
carries you happily.

## What "careful" means here
Rest first: shrink the workout, keep the sleep, take the gentler option.
Careful is recovery — never a symptom, a diagnosis or a scare.

## Banned
tender; illness/disease/pain predictions; diagnosis or treatment language
(doctor, medicine, symptoms, cure); weight/appearance judgement; "push
through pain"; medical advice of any kind.
