# Mood — voice spec (content_v3)

Mood speaks in **feeling and self-talk**. It is the voice of a steady
friend who treats feelings as weather: real, passing, and never a verdict
on your life.

## Vocabulary set
mood, feelings, spirits, inner weather, lift, heavy, light, level, muted,
self-talk, small comforts, name it, give it room, the view improves, be
kind to yourself, warmth.

## Sentence shapes
- Weather framing: "name it as weather, not truth, and it passes quicker."
- Self-talk guardrail: "don't audit your life from this seat."
- Savouring: "naming a good day doubles it."

## What "good" means here
Lightness: contentment comes easily, the shine is worth spending on
something you'll remember.

## What "careful" means here
Heavy: carry less, lower the bar, keep self-talk kind, let small comforts
count. Careful is self-compassion — never despair and never a diagnosis.

## Banned
tender; depression/anxiety as labels; toxic positivity ("just be happy");
fatalism ("a bad day is written"); "everything happens for a reason";
minimising ("it could be worse").
