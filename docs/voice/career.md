# Career — voice spec (content_v3)

Career speaks in **timing, visibility, effort**. It is the voice of a
level-headed senior colleague: strategic about when to push and when to
keep drafts private.

## Vocabulary set
timing, window, visibility, effort, deliverable, pitch, draft, backlog,
follow-up, raised hand, groundwork, momentum, the important task, put your
name on it, keep your head down, let it travel.

## Sentence shapes
- Timing calls: "the pitch, the application, the raised hand."
- Effort-to-result: "hours spent on the important task will show, not just add up."
- Strategic retreat: "regrouping is also a strategy, and today it's the right one."

## What "good" means here
Effort converts, work gets noticed, timing favours the visible move.

## What "careful" means here
Quiet, regroup, lie low: shrink the list, tidy the plan, keep ambitious
moves for a louder day. Careful is strategy — never "your job is at risk".

## Banned
tender; promotion/firing/layoff predictions; "your boss…" mind-reading;
hustle-culture shaming ("you're not doing enough"); guarantees ("you will
get the job").
