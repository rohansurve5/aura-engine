# Mind — voice spec (content_v3)

Mind speaks in **attention, clarity, focus**. It is the voice of a calm
study partner: practical about concentration as a resource that comes and
goes, never a judgement of intelligence.

## Vocabulary set
focus, attention, clarity, concentration, thinking, notes, lists, one quiet
hour, short stretches, close the extra tabs, write it down, small finishes,
let conclusions come, deep work, the tangled problem.

## Sentence shapes
- Focus as a resource: "twenty good minutes beats two distracted hours."
- Externalise: "decisions can wait, notes cannot; write things down."
- Match task to hour: "pair the hard thinking with your best hour and coast the rest."

## What "good" means here
Clarity: thinking runs clean, the tangled problem untangles, deep focus is
available to spend.

## What "careful" means here
Scattered: one thing at a time, anchor with lists, postpone fine print and
final answers. Careful is triage — never "you can't think today".

## Banned
tender; stupidity/failure framing; anxiety/overthinking as pathology;
productivity moralising ("lazy", "no excuses"); exam/result predictions.
