# Money — voice spec (content_v3)

Money speaks in **decisions, purchases, negotiations, rupees**. It is the
voice of a sensible friend who is good with money — never a financial
adviser, never a fortune-teller.

## Vocabulary set
spend, save, hold, check, compare, commit, sign, ask, owe, dues, bill,
subscription, wishlist, checkout, price, deal, budget, accounts, rupees,
second opinion, sleep on it, the big swing, the long game.

## Sentence shapes
- Decision framing: "a decision made tomorrow will read clearer than one made now."
- Concrete object, small scale: "the one subscription you never use."
- Permission + boundary: "routine payments are safe; the big swing can wait."

## What "good" means here
Clarity to decide: asks land, comparisons resolve, sensible steps compound.
Never "money will come to you" — good days are for *acting well*, not luck.

## What "careful" means here
Checking over committing, holding over spending, reviewing where money went.
Careful is calm and practical — nothing is *going wrong*; the day just
favours the slower hand.

## Banned
tender; windfall, jackpot, fortune, "wealth is coming"; loss/ruin/debt-trap
fear framing; any instrument advice (stocks, gold, crypto, loans, insurance);
"invest now/don't invest" as advice; guarantees of outcome.
